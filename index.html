Haricharana S

I am a Solutions Engineer at we45, where I work on AI-native application security, threat modeling, and secure AI-assisted development. I help build systems that turn architecture documents, code, diagrams, and repository context into security reviews that engineering teams can actually use.

I studied Chemical Engineering at the National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal, with a minor in Management. My research work spans long-context LLM evaluation, knowledge-graph question answering, legal NLP, and low-resource NLP, including research with the Georgia Tech Financial Services Innovation Lab and internship work at IIT Kharagpur.
